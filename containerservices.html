Container Services

Sea Freight

We specialise in sea freight services that are reliable, cost-effective and focused on providing professionally handled, customer-oriented shipment solutions. Our expertise encompasses every aspect, starting from complex documentations and clearances to real-time tracking. By utilising an established transportation network which consists of several trusted partners, we ensure that clients receive their goods on time and in a secure condition.

NVOCC

As a Non Vessel Owner Common Carrier, we assist importers and exporters in moving goods from point-to-point by identifying trade lanes and making informed decisions of pricing, warehousing and distribution.

ISO Tanks / Flexi-tanks / Hazardous Cargo

We are experienced in handling food grade chemicals, along with hazardous and non-hazardous liquid, powder and gaseous substances. For these purposes, we utilise ISO tanks, flexi-tanks, and dry bulk containers, adhering strictly to quality standards. We keep abreast with the latest technology, compliance, safety norms and regulations in order to ensure the safe and secure transport of hazardous cargo, every step of the way.

Multi-modal Transportation

Our door-to-door movement of cargo utilises rail, road, sea, and air, with the aim of reducing cost and maximising the reach and competitiveness of the goods in the market.
